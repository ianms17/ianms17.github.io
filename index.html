Teaching

Teaching, to me, is one of the most important things you can do. As a teacher, you have the unique opportunity to profoundly change somebody's life. At Texas A&M, I'm a Peer Teacher in the department of engineering. I help to teach a class called ENGR 102. The class is an introduction to programming for engineering students, most of whom have no programming experience at all. Using Python, the students go all the way from doing basic math operations without any variables to writing out full functions, doing complex math with NumPy, and graphing with Matplotlib, and I'm there to help them every step of the way. My job, primarily, is to help facilitate the lab portion of course. This involves answering questions about the assignments, debugging code, and helping students whenever the get stuck. I also help the professor with grading, and hold office hours to help the students outside of class. I really love the job, I find it very fulfilling, and I hope to keep on doing it until I graduate.
